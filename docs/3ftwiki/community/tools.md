# Social Media

Social media serves as a powerful conduit for sharing stories, mobilizing supporters, and fostering a united community. The best platforms will depend on your local community, but here are a few to get started.

**General Best Practices:**

- **Consistent Posting**: Regular updates keep your community engaged and informed.

- **Visual Content**: Utilize images and videos to convey compelling stories.

- **Community Interaction**: Respond to comments and messages to build trust and foster meaningful connections.

These established platforms are useful for sharing updates, but consider using an aggreagation service for posting (see below).

## [Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/)
If you have an active local subreddit in your area, it can be a useful tool for promoting events and recruiting community members to participate in local government surveys, sign petitions, and attend meetings.

###### Best Practices

- **Community Engagement**: Participate in relevant subreddits to share information and foster discussions about cycling advocacy.

- **Authentic Interaction**: Engage genuinely with users to build trust and credibility within the community.

## [Discord](https://discord.com/)
A communication platform offering voice, video, and text channels, ideal for building dedicated communities.

###### Best Practices

- **Create a Server**: Establish a dedicated server for your organization to facilitate real-time discussions and updates.

- **Organize Channels**: Set up specific channels for different topics, such as event planning, general discussions, and resource sharing.

# Email Marketing Services

Direct communication through email remains a cornerstone for engaging supporters and driving action.

## [Mailchimp](https://mailchimp.com/)
An email marketing platform offering customizable templates and automation features.

- **Pricing**: Free for up to 500 subscribers; paid plans start at $13 per month.

- **Non-Profit Discount**: Mailchimp offers a 15% discount for non-profit organizations.

######   Best Practices:

- **Segmentation**: Organize your audience based on interests to send targeted content.

- **Automation**: Set up automated welcome emails for new subscribers.

## [MailerLite](https://www.mailerlite.com/)
An intuitive email marketing service with robust features.

- **Pricing**: Free for up to 1,000 subscribers; paid plans start at $10 per month.

- **Non-Profit Discount**: MailerLite offers a 30% discount for non-profits.

######   Best Practices:

- **A/B Testing**: Experiment with different subject lines to optimize open rates.

- **Landing Pages**: Create dedicated pages to capture new subscribers.

## [ActiveCampaign](https://www.activecampaign.com/)
A platform combining email marketing, automation, and customer relationship management (CRM).

- **Pricing**: Plans start at $9 per month.

- **Non-Profit Discount**: Offers a 20% discount for non-profit organizations.

######   Best Practices:

- **Personalization**: Use CRM data to craft personalized messages that resonate with individual supporters.

- **A/B Testing**: Experiment with different email elements to determine what drives the best engagement.

# Graphic Design

## [Canva](https://www.canva.com/)

Canva is a user-friendly graphic design platform that enables users to create a wide array of visual content, including social media graphics, presentations, posters, and more.
- Pricing: Free basic version available
- Non-Profit Discount: Canva offers its premium features for free to registered nonprofits, allowing up to 50 team members to collaborate.

###### Best Practices:
- **Template Utilization:** Leverage Canva's extensive library of templates tailored for various platforms and purposes to maintain consistency and save time.
- **Brand Kit:** Use the Brand Kit feature to store your organization's logos, colors, and fonts, ensuring brand consistency across all designs.
- **Collaboration:** Invite team members to collaborate on designs in real-time, facilitating seamless feedback and edits.

## [Adobe Express](https://www.adobe.com/express/)
Adobe Express, formerly known as Adobe Spark, is a web-based design tool that offers a range of templates and design elements suitable for creating marketing materials.

- Pricing: Free basic plan available; premium plan at $10 per month includes additional features and assets.

###### Best Practices:
- **AI Integration:** Utilize Adobe Express's AI-powered features to simplify the design process, allowing for quick creation of professional-quality visuals.
- **Template Customization:** Start with pre-designed templates and customize them to align with your organization's branding and messaging.

## [Piktochart](https://piktochart.com/)

Piktochart is an easy-to-use design tool focused on creating infographics, presentations, and reports, making complex data more accessible and engaging.

- Pricing: Free plan available with basic features; premium plans offer advanced functionalities.

- Non-Profit Discount: Piktochart offers discounts for nonprofit organizations upon application.

###### Best Practices:
- Data Visualization: Utilize Piktochart to transform statistics and information into visually appealing infographics that effectively communicate your message.

- Customizable Templates: Select from a variety of templates and customize them to fit your organization's branding and the specific data you wish to present.

# Event Management

Events are the heartbeat of your community development, bringing people together to celebrate, educate, and mobilize.

## [Facebook Events](https://www.facebook.com/events/)
A straightforward way to create and promote events to your followers. Free to use.
###### Best Practices:

- Invitations: Personally invite your followers and encourage them to spread the word.

- Updates: Keep attendees engaged with regular updates and reminders.

## [Mobilizon](https://mobilizon.us/)
An open-source platform focused on privacy for organizing events and managing groups. Free to use.

###### Best Practices:

- Event Creation: Set up detailed events and share them within your community.

- Privacy Focused: Engage with members in a space that respects their data and autonomy.

- Federated Networking: Connect with other Mobilizon instances to expand your event's reach.

## [Partiful](https://partiful.com/)
A modern invitation platform designed for organizing social events with ease. Free to use.

###### Best Practices:
- Event Details: Provide comprehensive information to ensure attendees are well-prepared.
- Reminders: Utilize automated reminders to keep your event top-of-mind and reduce no-shows.

## [Mixily](https://www.mixily.com/)
Mixily offers a user-friendly platform designed to simplify event creation, promotion, and ticketing, making it an excellent choice for both virtual and in-person gatherings.

###### Key Features:
- **Intuitive Event Creation:** Set up events swiftly with customizable templates, allowing you to add images, detailed descriptions, and integrate seamlessly with platforms like Zoom for virtual meetups.

- **Efficient Ticketing System:** Manage ticket sales directly through Mixily, offering various pricing tiers and promotional codes to accommodate different attendee needs.

- **Automated Communication:** Keep your attendees informed with scheduled reminders and updates, ensuring they have all necessary information leading up to the event.

###### Pricing:
- Free Plan: Ideal for basic event setups without ticketing requirements.
- Ticketed Events: A fee of 1% plus $0.30 per paid ticket is applied, with additional credit card processing fees.

###### Best Practices:

- Engaging Invitations: Utilize Mixily's customizable templates to create visually appealing invitations that capture the essence of your event.

- Streamlined RSVPs: Simplify the RSVP process, allowing attendees to confirm their participation with ease, enhancing overall event attendance.

- Data-Driven Planning: Leverage Mixily's analytics to gain insights into attendee engagement and ticket sales, informing future event strategies.

# Document Collaboration
Effective collaboration is the cornerstone of successful advocacy. Utilizing tools that allow multiple contributors to edit and refine documents in real-time ensures that your press releases, letters to council, and other vital communications are polished and impactful.

## [Google Docs](https://docs.google.com/)

A widely-used platform that enables real-time collaboration on documents, spreadsheets, and presentations.
- Pricing: Free for individuals; included in Google Workspace for organizations.
- Non-Profit Discount: Eligible nonprofits can access Google Workspace for free.

###### Best Practices:
- Real-Time Editing: Collaborate simultaneously with team members, ensuring immediate input and feedback.

- Commenting and Suggesting: Utilize these features to propose changes and discuss edits without altering the original text.

## [Microsoft 365](https://www.office.com/)

Offers robust document editing with seamless integration across Microsoft applications.
- Pricing: Nonprofit plans start at $2 per user per month.

###### Best Practices:
- Version Control: Track changes and access previous versions to monitor document evolution.
- Integration: Leverage integration with other Microsoft tools like Teams for enhanced collaboration.

## [Notion](https://www.notion.so/)

An all-in-one workspace combining note-taking, project management, and collaborative document editing.

- Pricing: Free for personal use; Plus Plan at $10 per member per month.
- Non-Profit Discount: Certified 501(c)(3) nonprofits in the U.S. can access the Plus Plan at a 50% discount, reducing the cost to $5 per member per month.

###### Best Practices:

- Centralized Information: Create a centralized hub for all organizational documents, ensuring easy access and collaboration.

- Templates: Utilize customizable templates for consistent document formatting and structure.

## [Etherpad](https://etherpad.org/)

An open-source, web-based collaborative real-time editor, ideal for teams seeking a straightforward, no-frills editing experience. Free and open-source.

###### Best Practices:
- Simplicity: Use Etherpad for quick, real-time collaboration without the need for complex features.

- Customization: Host your own instance to maintain control over data and tailor the platform to your needs.
